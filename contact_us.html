Contact & Support

This page is reserved exclusively for formal complaints and technical support requests related to Award Studios projects, services, or digital assets. Please ensure that your message contains a clear subject line, a concise description of the issue, and any relevant attachments or screenshots.

Service Hours: 07:00 to 24:00 (North America Time Zone). Messages received outside this timeframe will be queued and addressed during the next available service period.

For technical support, you can reach us only at: support.awardstudios@gmail.com

Any communication that is frivolous, irrelevant, abusive, or offensive will be classified as SPAM and disregarded. Award Studios maintains strict internal protocols to ensure that all valid inquiries are processed with professionalism and confidentiality.
