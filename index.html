Contact & Support

This communication channel is reserved exclusively for formal complaints and technical support requests related to Award Studios projects, services, or digital assets. In order to maintain operational clarity and ensure that every legitimate inquiry receives the appropriate level of attention, strict guidelines govern the use of this contact form and associated email addresses. Service Hours All complaints and technical support requests will be processed only during official service hours: 07:00 to 24:00 (North America Time Zone). Messages received outside this timeframe will be queued and addressed during the next available service period. This schedule ensures that our support team can operate with consistency, accountability, and professional oversight. Scope of Accepted Messages Only messages that contain a clear description of a technical issue, malfunction, or formal complaint will be acknowledged. Each submission must include: - A precise subject line that identifies the nature of the issue. - A concise but complete description of the problem. - Relevant attachments or screenshots, when applicable. - A preferred contact email for follow‑up. Messages that do not meet these criteria may be disregarded without response. Policy on Irrelevant or Offensive Content Any communication that is frivolous, irrelevant, abusive, or offensive will be classified as SPAM. Such submissions will not be acknowledged, and the originating email address will be subject to immediate blocking. This measure is necessary to protect the integrity of our support system and to ensure that legitimate users receive timely assistance. Repeated attempts to send disruptive or offensive content may result in permanent exclusion from all Award Studios communication channels. Operational Standards Award Studios maintains strict internal protocols for complaint handling and technical support. Each valid inquiry is logged, assigned a reference number, and processed according to established escalation procedures. Responses are issued within a maximum of three business days, unless the complexity of the issue requires extended investigation. All communications are handled with professionalism, confidentiality, and adherence to applicable legal standards. Consent and Data Processing By submitting a complaint or support request, you consent to the processing of your information solely for the purpose of resolving your inquiry. Award Studios does not use contact data for marketing or unrelated purposes. All information is stored securely and handled in compliance with international data protection principles. you can reach us for technical support only at: support.awardstudios@gmail.com

About

Summary Award Studios is a studio specialized in the preservation, restoration, and reinterpretation of digital and cultural experiences with a rigorous, professional approach. Born from the intersection of historical research, software engineering, and creative design, the studio is dedicated to conserving interactive heritage and producing projects that respect the original integrity while incorporating technical and accessibility improvements. Our work combines academic methods with industrial practices to deliver reproducible, documented, and sustainable outcomes. Each project is treated as a cultural and technical responsibility, governed by clear standards of quality and traceability. The mission of Award Studios is to safeguard and revitalize significant digital works so that present and future generations can access them with fidelity and context. We document processes, preserve assets, and create versions that retain the essential experience of the original without compromising security or legality. We also educate communities and collaborators on best practices for digital preservation, promoting policies and tools that facilitate long-term conservation. Our commitment is to authenticity, transparency, and technical excellence. We aim to establish replicable protocols, asset libraries, and frameworks that enable cultural institutions, developers, and collectors to collaborate securely and efficiently. We envision an ecosystem where interactive heritage is managed with professional criteria, where documentation and public access coexist with respect for rights and licenses. Award Studios aspires to be a bridge between digital memory and responsible innovation. Services Preservation and Archiving We implement capture, cataloging, and storage processes for digital assets with comprehensive metadata and file formats chosen for longevity. Our strategies include redundancy and verification to minimize the risk of data loss. Approach and Methodology Our approach combines methodological rigor with cultural sensitivity. Each project begins with documentary and technical research to establish the original state and intervention priorities. We apply reproducible methodologies: version control, automated testing, change logs, and open documentation. Ethics are central: we assess copyrights, licenses, and permissions, prioritizing solutions that respect creators and rights holders. Technically, we favor open standards and non‑proprietary formats when feasible, and we adopt preservation practices recognized by the professional community. We also integrate accessibility and usability criteria to broaden the reach of restored works. Closing Award Studios operates under the conviction that digital preservation is a collective responsibility requiring professionalism, transparency, and collaboration. If your institution, collection, or project needs a serious, technically sound solution to conserve, restore, or present digital heritage, Award Studios is a partner committed to quality and sustainability. We deliver clear deliverables, complete documentation, and a legally informed approach that protects both asset owners and audiences. For formal inquiries, collaboration proposals, or budget requests, we are available to evaluate your case with the technical and curatorial attention it deserves..
