About

Award Studios is a studio specialized in the preservation, restoration, and reinterpretation of digital and cultural experiences with a rigorous, professional approach. Our mission is to safeguard and revitalize significant digital works so that present and future generations can access them with fidelity and context. We operate under the conviction that digital preservation is a collective responsibility requiring professionalism, transparency, and collaboration.
